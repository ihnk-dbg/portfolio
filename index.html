Case studies and production systems ‚Äî design, tech decisions, and impact.

My approach to building production-grade systems blends pragmatic architecture with measurable outcomes. I start with focused discovery to align on goals and constraints, then design for observability, scalability, and clear ownership. Prototypes validate trade-offs early; thorough testing, CI/CD automation, and SLO-driven monitoring ensure reliable delivery. I emphasise clear documentation and smooth handoffs so teams can operate and iterate confidently.

  • Discovery-led design: short, focused spikes to validate assumptions and reduce risk.
  • Operational-first: telemetry, alerting, and dashboards baked in from day one.
  • Trade-offs documented: architecture notes explaining why a technology was chosen and what was avoided.
  • Impact metrics: examples include latency reduction, error-rate improvements, and reduced toil through automation.
